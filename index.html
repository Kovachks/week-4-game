Pizza Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Ninja Turtles below. By clicking on a Ninja Turtle you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random numbers, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each Ninja Turtle is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
